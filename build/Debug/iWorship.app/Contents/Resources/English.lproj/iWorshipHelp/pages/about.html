About ProWorship

ProWorship is a new breed of worship presentation software for the Mac that combines incredible ease of use with rock solid stability and powerful features. The slick and intuitive interface delivers a familiar experience that's right at home on your Mac.

Key Features:

■

Written from the ground up to be completely Mac OS X native.

■

Universal binary running on both Intel and PowerPC systems.

■

Slick, intuitive interface with virtually no learning curve, even for the beginner. Training volunteers to use the program is a breeze!

■

High-Definition video playback support.

■

Live video support via any connected FireWire camera. Stream live video of your worship set directly behind your lyrics without needing an expensive hardware solution.

■

DVD playback and full control directly within ProWorship. Show a movie clip directly from a DVD without having to use a seperate program.

■

Instantly adapts to your playback monitor with zero configuration required.

■

Blazing fast editing - easily edit slides by double cliking directly in the thumbnails.

■

Live spell check of your slides happen automatically as you type. No more embarassing mis-spelled slides in the middle of your service.

■

Keep your library safe by backing up to your .Mac iDisk directly within ProWorship.

When setting out to create ProWorship, the most important goal was stability and an intuitive interface. Even though there are a handful of worship programs available for the Mac most of them are too complex and unintuitive for the average computer user to just sit down and figure out. What would it look like if software was available that was both feature-rich and easy enough for the beginner to learn? It was with these goals in mind that ProWorship was created.
