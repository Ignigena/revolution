Recommended Video Codecs

Using the right video codec is key to smooth video playback in ProWorship. Many purchased videos come automatically encoded in MPEG or Animation codecs. Playing these videos will almost always produce choppy video playback on all but the fastest machines.

When encoding your video there is a fine line between quality and file size. You want your video to be high quality, but if you encode it using Animation or another lossless encoding your video file size will be huge and force your hard drive to work very hard. On the other hand, if you encode your video with a codec like H.264 that compresses the file size, your graphics card will have to work very hard. In either situation, this will most likely lead to poor playback quality.

In our own independent testing, we have found the following encoding settings to be a good balance between file size and video quality and should produce good playback quality on most systems.

Quicktime Encoding Settings:

■

Compression type: Photo - JPEG

■

Frame rate/keyframe: default/automatic

■

Data rate: default/automatic

■

Quality: around 75%

Feel free to experiment around with your own settings and let us know if you find a better solution.
